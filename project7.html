Introduction Of ModerMart App

Modern Mart is India's No.1 mobile skin brand and a leading manufacturer and wholesaler of high-quality mobile and laptop laminations, cleaning solutions, lamination tools, and cutting machines. With over 1 lakh+ satisfied customers, we take pride in offering the most advanced plotter machines and India’s first Android-based mobile skin cutting app. Our product lineup includes mobile skin cutting machines, lamination heat guns, 2-layer side tape, and a wide array of vinyl-based mobile skin materials designed for long-lasting protection and superior aesthetics. Powered by Modern Mart Skin & Plotter Android software, our integrated system offers unlimited skin designs, a custom photo print feature, and model-based cutting templates – all accessible from your mobile phone. With 42,000+ device templates spanning over 50 smartphone brands, our app is revolutionizing the skin-cutting industry with daily updates, unmatched customization, and seamless plotter connectivity.

Problem Solved By ModerMart App

Before Modern Mart:

    Retailers had to rely on pre-cut skins, which often didn’t fit well or lacked personalization. There was no affordable, all-in-one solution combining software and hardware. Device templates were limited, and software lacked regular updates. End-users struggled to find unique, well-fitting skins for newer devices.

With Modern Mart:

    Users get perfect-fit skins instantly through real-time model cutting previews. Our app supports custom image uploads, enabling true personalization. One-click connectivity with the plotter ensures high-precision cuts. Our extensive template library ensures compatibility with almost every device. Retailers gain full control and flexibility with low operational costs.

Challenges Faced While Building the ModernMart App

Plotter-App Connectivity: One of the biggest technical challenges was establishing seamless communication between the mobile app and the plotter machine, especially via Bluetooth and OTG. Ensuring real-time command execution and zero lag cutting required deep firmware optimization and app-hardware integration.
